Research Projects

Multi-Person 3D Pose Estimation and Tracking from Multiple View

  • Associating human keypoints across multiple cameras and frames to reconstruct and track people in 3D space, robust to single-camera occlusion and working in real-time.
  • Screenshots, Video Demo

Online / Near-Online Multi-Object Tracking

  • A series of tracking-by-detection approaches is proposed for tracking multiple people in a monocular, unconstrained camera, achieved state-of-the-art performance on MOTChallenge and KITTI benchmarks for both accuracy and processing speed.
  • Paper1, Paper2, Code, Video Demo

Single Camera Depth Estimation with Synthetic Data

  • Obtianing relative depth from single image, trained using both real-world images from NYU Depth and synthetic images from the computer game GTA-V.
  • Video1, Video2, Online Demo

Object Detection in Faster R-CNN and YOLO

  • Learning and implementing Faster R-CNN and YOLOv2 in PyTorch, which is the first object detector, or one of the firsts, implemented in PyTorch.
  • Codes of Faster R-CNN, YOLOv2
